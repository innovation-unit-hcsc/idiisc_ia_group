Mission & scope

A common space for AI in healthcare at IdISSC

The AI Working Group, led by the Scientific Director Dr. Julio Mayol, aims to map existing AI-related activity across the institute and foster new collaborations. Our objectives are to:

01 · Identify

Identify ongoing and planned research projects that apply Artificial Intelligence in any area of the Hospital Clínico San Carlos and IdISSC.

02 · Connect

Connect clinicians, data scientists and technical teams, facilitating multidisciplinary collaborations around common problems and datasets.

03 · Accelerate

Support the design of robust, clinically relevant and ethically sound AI projects that can scale and integrate into the healthcare system.
